Pushpa 2: The Rule

2024 upcoming...

The Rule is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action drama film
directed and written by Sukumar. It is produced by Mythri Movie Makers
and Sukumar Writings. The film stars Allu Arjun in the titular
role alongside Fahadh Faasil and Rashmika Mandanna. It is the second
instalment in the Pushpa film series and the sequel to the 2021 film Pushpa: The Rise
